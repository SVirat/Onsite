# Privacy Policy for Onsite

**Last updated: June 26, 2026**

## Summary

Onsite does **not** collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal or sensitive
user data. The extension runs entirely within your browser.

## Information We Store

Onsite stores only your feature preferences locally on your own device using the
browser's `chrome.storage.local` API:

- Which features are toggled on or off (difficulty, acceptance/stats, social,
  topics blindfolds, and the master on/off switch).
- The interview timer duration setting.
- The run-limit setting and the per-problem run count.
- The detected LeetCode theme, used only so the popup matches the page.

This information never leaves your browser, is not sent to any server, and is not
shared with the developer or any third party.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

Onsite does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

- Personally identifiable information (name, email, address, etc.).
- Authentication information, passwords, or credentials.
- Browsing history or web activity outside of the LeetCode pages it operates on.
- Your code, submissions, or solutions.
- Location, financial, or health information.

## Tracking and Third Parties

Onsite does not use analytics, tracking, cookies, advertising, or remote code.
It does not communicate with any external servers. It operates solely on LeetCode
pages (`leetcode.com` and `leetcode.cn`) to modify the page's appearance for
interview-style practice.

## Permissions

- **`storage`** — used to save your settings locally so they persist between
  sessions and so the per-problem run count cannot be bypassed by reloading.
- **Host access to LeetCode domains** (`leetcode.com`, `leetcode.cn`) — required
  because the extension's function is to modify the LeetCode page in your browser.
  Access is scoped strictly to these domains and no other sites.

## Data Retention and Deletion

All stored settings remain on your device until you delete them. You can clear
them at any time by removing the extension from your browser, which deletes its
local storage.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published in this file with a
new "Last updated" date.
